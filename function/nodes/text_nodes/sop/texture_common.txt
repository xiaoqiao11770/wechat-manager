div id="layerTip">>
    TIP:
        Use a [Layer|Node:sop/layer] operation in conjunction with this
        operation to create multiple sets of texture coordinates.

div id="visualizePara">>
    The effect of this operation is best visualized in the UV viewport,
    or with textures turned on in the 3D viewport.

    XYZ:
        Corresponds to distance on the geometry.
    UVW:
        Corresponds to distance in UVW (texture) space.
    UV:
        Ignores the third dimension in texture space.
