= VEX Attenuated Spotlight =

"""An attenuated cone light source."""

An attenuated cone light source. The attenuation specifies the distance
at which the intensity of the light source is half of that at the light
origin.

The cone rolloff changes the distribution of illumination over the umbra
of the cone
