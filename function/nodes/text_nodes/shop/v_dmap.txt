= VEX Displace Map =

"""This shader will displace the surface geometry based on the luminance
of the texture map."""

This shader will displace the surface geometry based on the luminance of
the texture map. If texture coordinates found, they will be used.
Otherwise, the surface parametric coordinates will be used.
