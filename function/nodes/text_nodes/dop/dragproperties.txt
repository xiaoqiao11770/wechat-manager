= Drag Properties =

"""Defines how the surrounding medium affects a soft body object."""

The Cloth Collision Properties DOP defines how a soft body object
is affected by the velocity of the surrounding medium, such as air or water.
It is applied automatically as subdata to a [Cloth Object|Node:dop/clothobject].
