= Source Relationship =

The Source Relationship DOP is used by the Smoke and Fluid solvers to have
another simulation object act as a [smoke or fluid source|/shelf/sourcefromvolume] for the [smoke|Node:dop/smokeobject] or [fluid object|Node:dop/fluidobject].
