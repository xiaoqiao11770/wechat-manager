= Make Transform =

"""Builds a general 4x4 transform matrix."""

This operator builds a general 4x4 transform matrix given an order of
transformations (`trs`), an order for rotations (`xyz`), vectors
representing the translation (`t`), rotation (`r`) and scale (`s`) amounts,
and optionally a pivot.
