= Rotate =

"""Applies a rotation by 'angle' radians to the given 3x3 or 4x4
matrix."""

This operator applies a rotation by `angle` radians to the given 3x3 or
4x4 matrix. `axis` specifies the axis of rotation, which can be an
arbitrary direction and must be normalized.
