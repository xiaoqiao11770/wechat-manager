= Bricker =

"""Generates a brick pattern based on the parametric `s` and `t`
coordinates."""

Generates a brick pattern based on the parametric `s` and `t` coordinates.

If the texture coordinate inputs (s and t) are not connected, the global
variables by the same names will be used instead.
