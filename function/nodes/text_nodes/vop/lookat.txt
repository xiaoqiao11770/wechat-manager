= Look At =

"""Computes a 3x3 rotation matrix to orient the z-axis along the vector
(to - from) under the transformation."""

This operator computes a 3x3 rotation matrix to orient the z-axis along
the vector (to - from) under the transformation. If an up vector is
specified, it will determine the roll.
